Compiled 2026-07-10. **Methodology note:** WebFetch (direct page retrieval) was non-functional for this entire research session — it returned HTTP 403 on every target tested, including neutral control URLs (e.g., en.wikipedia.org), confirming a session-level tool issue rather than site-specific bot-blocking. All facts below were retrieved via WebSearch, which performs its own page fetch + synthesis and returns source URLs. I did not independently render the raw HTML of vendor pricing pages. Where numbers vary across sources, that variance is reported rather than silently resolved. Treat pricing figures as **directionally reliable, not receipt-verified** — re-confirm against the live pricing pages before publishing a price on our comparison pages, especially Jobber's individual-vs-team plan split.

## Jobber

**Pricing (as reported by multiple secondary sources; official getjobber.com/pricing returned 403 on direct fetch):**
- Core: $29/mo (billed annually) / $39/mo (billed monthly) — 1 user
- Connect: reported as ~$119–129/mo (some sources frame as an individual-tier price) and separately as $169/mo monthly ($129/mo annual) for a 5-user team tier — **sources are inconsistent on individual vs. team framing; verify directly before publishing**
- Grow: ~$199/mo (monthly) / ~$249/mo (annual, 10 users per some sources)
- Plus: ~$599/mo (monthly) / ~$449/mo (annual, 15 users per some sources)
- Additional users beyond a team plan's included count: ~$29/mo/user (per one source)
- Trial: **14-day free trial**, no long-term contract, consistently reported across sources.
- Source: [Jobber Pricing 2026: Core, Connect, Grow Plans Explained](https://buyersprint.com/2026/04/17/jobber-pricing-2026/); [Jobber Prices and Plans Breakdown](https://www.getonecrew.com/post/jobber-prices); official page title snippet "Jobber Pricing: Plans Starting at $29/Month | Free Trial" at [getjobber.com/pricing](https://www.getjobber.com/pricing/) (title/meta only — full page 403'd on fetch). Accessed 2026-07-10.

**AI Receptionist ("Receptionist – powered by Jobber AI"):**
- What it does: answers inbound calls and texts 24/7 in the shop's voice; can offer to book a visit, capture request details, or take a message; texts back callers who hang up; recognizes returning callers by name if they exist in Jobber CRM; can be told what to listen for and will text the owner or transfer the live call on trigger phrases; handles multiple simultaneous calls; full call/text log with real-time notifications in the Jobber dashboard.
- What it does NOT do (per third-party analysis, not Jobber's own claim — flag as inference): calls/texts only — no WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, email, or web-chat-widget coverage; works only inside the Jobber ecosystem (no cross-platform booking); one source frames it as call/text capture + basic booking, not full back-office automation (no voice-issued invoicing, no estimate drafting via Receptionist itself — that lives in a separate "Jobber AI Voice and Chat (Beta)" mobile-app feature, see below).
- Pricing: **included unlimited on the Plus plan**; add-on to other select plans at **$29/mo for 30 conversations**, then **$0.79 per additional conversation** over the cap.
- Sources: [AI Receptionist for Home Service Businesses](https://www.getjobber.com/features/ai-receptionist/); [Receptionist–powered by Jobber AI (Help Center)](https://help.getjobber.com/hc/en-us/articles/25315927533847-Receptionist-powered-by-Jobber-AI); [Jobber Launches AI-Powered Receptionist (PR Newswire)](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/jobber-launches-ai-powered-receptionist-to-answer-calls-and-texts-for-busy-home-service-businesses-302531125.html); [Jobber AI Receptionist HVAC gap analysis (competitor content, third-party)](https://reliablereceptionist.com/jobber-ai-receptionist-hvac-integration-gap/). Accessed 2026-07-10.

**Separate feature — "Jobber AI Voice and Chat (Beta)":** an in-app assistant (not the phone receptionist) that lets an admin user create quotes, send invoices, check schedules, and capture field details hands-free via voice inside the mobile app, plus a chat mode for business-coaching Q&A and marketing-content generation. Available on all plans for admin users; non-admins get chat-only product support. Source: [Jobber AI Voice and Chat (Beta) – Help Center](https://help.getjobber.com/hc/en-us/articles/25315900454423-Jobber-AI-Voice-and-Chat-Beta). Accessed 2026-07-10.

**Target segment:** solo operators through ~15-person teams (Plus plan capped around 15 included users); positioned as the small-to-mid-market default across HVAC, plumbing, lawn care, cleaning, and other home-service trades.

## Housecall Pro

**Pricing (official housecallpro.com/pricing returned 403 on direct fetch; corroborated across several secondary breakdowns):**
- Basic: $79/mo (monthly) / $59/mo (annual) — 1 user
- Essentials: $189/mo (monthly) / $149/mo (annual) — up to 5 users
- MAX: $329/mo (monthly) / $299/mo (annual) — additional users $35/mo each
- One source cites lower "Basic $49 / Essential $129 (5 users)" figures — **conflicts with the $79/$189/$329 figures above; the pricing-page meta snippet itself reads "From $59/mo," consistent with the annual Basic price** — treat $59–79 (Basic) / $149–189 (Essentials) / $299–329 (MAX) as the reconcilable band and verify exact current numbers directly.
- Trial: **14 days free, full MAX-plan feature access, no credit card required to start**, cancel anytime; billing reminder sent before trial ends.
- Sources: [Housecall Pro Pricing & Plans (title/meta)](https://www.housecallpro.com/pricing/); [Housecall Pro Pricing 2026: 3 Plans from $59–$299/user/month](https://costbench.com/software/field-service-management/housecall-pro/); [Jobber Alternatives comparison citing Housecall Pro at $49/$129](https://foreman.co/blog/jobber-alternatives-small-contractors) (conflicting figure, noted above). Accessed 2026-07-10.

**AI features — "CSR AI" (part of the "AI Team" feature set):**
- What it does: answers calls and chats 24/7, engages customers with empathetic responses, asks clarifying questions, answers common questions; customizable answering/booking behavior and voice; February 2026 update added custom call-reason tagging (New Job Inquiry, Emergency Repair, Follow-Up, Sales Call) for revenue-source tracking.
- What it does NOT do yet: **job booking is explicitly listed as "coming soon"** as of the reviewed material — i.e., CSR AI answers and qualifies but autonomous booking is not yet fully shipped per this source. This is a meaningful gap vs. a fully closed-loop AI receptionist.
- Third-party AI voice add-ons also integrate with Housecall Pro (Kickcall, AgentZap, Smith.ai, Rosie, Goodcall) — these are external vendors, not Housecall Pro's own AI.
- Sources: [CSR AI Overview – Housecall Pro Help Center](https://help.housecallpro.com/en/articles/9740104-csr-ai-overview); [24/7 AI Customer Service Assistant](https://www.housecallpro.com/features/ai-team/csr-ai/); [Housecall Pro Feb 2026 Product Release](https://www.housecallpro.com/resources/february-2026-product-updates/). Accessed 2026-07-10.

**Target segment:** solo operators through small/mid teams; frequently recommended as the most direct "published pricing, straightforward interface" alternative to Jobber for small home-service trades.

## ServiceTitan

**Pricing:** ServiceTitan **does not publish pricing**; all figures below are third-party estimates from reseller/consultant blogs, not ServiceTitan's own site (servicetitan.com/pricing returned 403 on fetch and, per multiple secondary sources, shows no public numbers — sales-quote-only).
- Estimated $245–$500+ per technician per month across Starter/Essentials/Works-style tiers (naming varies by source).
- Setup/implementation fees estimated at $5,000–$50,000+, with 3–6 month (sometimes 12+ month) onboarding timelines reported.
- Contracts: **12+ month annual commitment reported as standard**, with early-termination fees cited at $5,000–$20,000+.
- Add-on modules (Marketing Pro, Phones Pro, Dispatch Pro, Fleet Pro, Pricebook Pro) priced separately; Marketing Pro estimated at $2,000+/mo, Phones Pro at $50–$200+/mo.
- No free trial — enterprise sales-demo model.
- Sources (all third-party estimates, not ServiceTitan-confirmed): [ServiceTitan Pricing 2026: Full Cost Breakdown](https://projul.com/blog/servicetitan-pricing-analysis-2026/); [ServiceTitan Pricing 2026: Real Costs Exposed ($150–$500+/Month)](https://fieldcamp.ai/reviews/servicetitan/); [ServiceTitan Pricing Per Month (2026)](https://myquoteiq.com/servicetitan-pricing-per-month/). Accessed 2026-07-10. **Mark as UNVERIFIED against a primary source** — do not publish these exact dollar figures as fact on our pages; safe to say "ServiceTitan does not publish pricing; third-party estimates place cost in the low-hundreds of dollars per technician per month plus five-figure setup fees and annual contracts," with the same citations.

**AI features — "Titan Intelligence" / "Atlas AI":**
- Titan Intelligence is ServiceTitan's umbrella AI initiative (launched 2023, expanded through 2026): Dispatch Pro auto-matches jobs to techs by skill/proximity/history/expected revenue; Scheduling Pro's "TI Chat Assistant" is a virtual agent trained on the business's own call recordings that can understand intent, collect contact info, book jobs, and answer questions; Atlas is a general AI copilot inside the ServiceTitan product surface.
- Sources: [Titan Intelligence (ti) is AI for the Trades](https://www.servicetitan.com/features/titan-intelligence); [The Future is Now: Titan Intelligence brings AI-powered features to ServiceTitan](https://www.servicetitan.com/blog/titan-intelligence-2023). Accessed 2026-07-10.

**Target segment:** consistently described as built for larger operations — "$1M+ in revenue," "20+ technicians," or businesses with dedicated office staff to manage Pricebook/configuration complexity; explicitly described across multiple independent buyer-guide sources as more than a 1–3-truck shop needs. One review-aggregator note: reviewers on that platform skew 92% small business by self-identification, construction 57% — but this conflicts with the "$1M+/20+ techs" framing from buyer guides and should be read as G2-style reviewer-composition data, not a claim that ServiceTitan targets micro shops. Source: [ServiceTitan Software Pricing, Alternatives & More 2026 (Capterra)](https://www.capterra.com/p/150053/ServiceTitan/). Accessed 2026-07-10.

## Industry stats — missed calls / admin hours

### Accepted (traceable, with caveats noted)

1. **27% of calls to home-services businesses (HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical) go unanswered**, and each missed call is estimated at **~$1,200 in lost revenue** for the trade. Attributed to Invoca, a call-tracking/analytics vendor, aggregating its own platform's inbound call data (reported dataset size: ~4.2M inbound calls/year, 2024–2026 rolling average). **Caveat: this is vendor-reported platform data from a company that sells call-tracking software, not an independently audited academic or government study — cite it as "Invoca, an ad-tech call analytics vendor, reports...", not as neutral third-party research.**
   Source: [Invoca — See How Much Missed Sales Calls Cost Home Services Businesses](https://www.invoca.com/blog/how-much-missed-sales-calls-cost-home-services-businesses). Accessed 2026-07-10.

2. **36% of an entrepreneur's work week goes to small administrative tasks** (invoicing, data entry, scheduling, ordering supplies, etc.); **31% spend a quarter to half their working hours on such tasks**. Survey of **251 U.S. entrepreneurs**, commissioned by Time Etc, reported independently by Forbes.
   Sources: [Forbes — New Survey Reveals Productivity Blind Spot For Entrepreneurs](https://www.forbes.com/sites/barnabylashbrooke/2023/11/28/new-survey-reveals-productivity-blind-spot-for-entrepreneurs/); [Time Etc — The Big Price Of Small Tasks](https://www.timeetc.com/resources/how-to-achieve-more/the-big-price-of-small-tasks-how-entrepreneurs-may-be-unwittingly-keeping-their-businesses-small/). Accessed 2026-07-10. Note: original survey year is 2023 (Forbes bylined Nov 2023); still the most traceable version of this stat as of 2026.

3. **33% of small business owners work more than 50 hours/week; 25% work more than 60 hours/week.** Cited by SCORE.org, sourcing a survey from The Alternative Board (TAB). **Caveat: SCORE is reporting on TAB's survey, not running its own — cite as "a survey from The Alternative Board, reported by SCORE.org."**
   Source: [SCORE.org — How Hard Small Business Owners Work](https://www.score.org/resource/blog-post/how-hard-small-business-owners-work); [The Alternative Board — Time Management survey](https://www.thealternativeboard.com/time-management). Accessed 2026-07-10.

4. **85% of callers who reach voicemail do not leave a message** and move on to a competitor. This figure recurs across multiple industry blogs (Invoca-adjacent and AI-answering-service vendor content) without a single traceable named study — **treat as directionally credible but UNVERIFIED against one primary source; use only as "widely reported in industry content," not as a hard-cited stat.**

### Rejected — could not trace to a primary source

- **"62% of small business calls go unanswered, costing $126,000/year"** — this pairing appears on dozens of AI-answering-service marketing sites (getaira.io, dialfyne.com, thebrobot.com, z360.biz, etc.), all pointing to each other or to vague attributions ("AMBS Call Center," a call-answering vendor, and a "411 Locals" study said to cover "85 businesses across 58 industries"). No single primary report could be located; the number is best understood as a recycled marketing statistic circulating through AI-receptionist-vendor content, not an independent study. **Do not publish this figure on our site.**
- **"$310,000/month in potentially lost revenue"** (from a hypothetical 100-calls/$5,000-avg-ticket scenario) — this is arithmetic from a blog's illustrative example, not a measured finding. **Do not publish as a stat.**
- **"HVAC contractors miss between 27% and 74% of incoming calls"** — the 74% upper bound has no identifiable source; only the 27% (Invoca) figure above is traceable. **Do not publish the 74% figure.**
