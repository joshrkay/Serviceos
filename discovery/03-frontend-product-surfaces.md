## Summary

The operator's "Next.js App Router" claim is false, but what actually exists is better than that claim implied: a disciplined React 18 + Vite SPA (562 TS/TSX files, ~54.7k LOC of non-test TSX, 247 co-located test files) with a single well-structured router (`web/routes.ts`, ~40 routes, per-route lazy chunks + manual vendor splitting), no Storybook/Chromatic (confirmed: no `.storybook/`, no dep). The hand-rolled data layer (`useListQuery`/`useDetailQuery`/`useMutation`/`useApiClient`/`streamAuth`) is genuinely strong — monotonic request-version guards, background-vs-foreground load semantics, fresh-Clerk-token-per-call with latched 401 sign-out — and the highest-stakes surfaces (inbox proposal approval with server-anchored undo, dispatch board with SSE + watchdog + optimistic concurrency + idempotency keys, Stripe payment pages) are the most hardened code in the repo. The onboarding funnel (signup → identity → pack → phone → billing → ai_check → test_call) is fully built with careful analytics dedup and server-derived resumability; the self-serve signup→live-voice-agent bet is real in code. The voice OS claim is ~80% real: Deepgram streaming STT via server-minted 30s grant tokens, a full WS gateway client, barge-in conversation loop — but the assistant chat itself is plain POST; the defined `assistant.token` streaming frames are never wired. The biggest structural risks are: a unit-test layer that mocks the network in 38 files while the real-API Playwright suite self-skips without full CI provisioning (contract drift is invisible by default); un-scoped `serviceos.*` localStorage that survives sign-out (cached permissions, message drafts, a technician impersonation override); and zero i18n on customer-facing pages despite Spanish being a headline voice feature. Estimates/Invoices pages are heavy copy-paste twins with a wasted list fetch on every detail deep-link. Overall: a Working→Hardened product surface with unusually good concurrency hygiene, whose weaknesses are testing truthfulness, duplication, and product-completeness gaps rather than correctness.
